Copied App is a menu bar clipboard manager. After install, its icon appears in the system menu bar — click it or press ⌃⇧C from any app to open the popover and view clipboard history. No sign-in required.

In-App Purchase:
iCloud Sync is a $4.99 non-consumable in-app purchase (product ID: com.magneton.copied.icloud_sync). Without it, clipboard history is stored locally only. To test the purchase flow:

1. Open Settings (⌘,) → Sync tab
2. Click "Unlock iCloud Sync"
3. Complete the purchase via a sandbox tester account
4. The app will prompt to quit and relaunch
5. On relaunch, iCloud Sync becomes active — confirmed by "Sync is active" indicator

"Restore Purchases" is available in the same Sync tab.

Permissions:
The app requests macOS Accessibility permission on first launch so the global hotkey (⌃⇧C) can be triggered from any app. This is optional; without it, the user opens the popover by clicking the menu bar icon.
