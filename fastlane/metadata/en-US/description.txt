Copied is a fast, menu-bar clipboard manager for macOS.

Every copy you make — text, images, files, code snippets, links — is saved automatically and searchable from a single keyboard shortcut. Pin your favorites, organize with lists, and paste rich content back with full fidelity.

FEATURES
• Fast menu bar popover with keyboard-first navigation
• Full-text search, including rich text and HTML bodies
• Smart content detection for links, code, images, and files
• Rich Copy As: write the exact pasteboard types your target app expects
• copied:// URL scheme for automation
• Automatic tracking-parameter stripping on copied URLs
• Favorites and trash with time-based retention
• Optional iCloud sync (in-app purchase) to keep your clipboard in sync across Macs

Designed for writers, developers, and anyone who lives on the clipboard.
