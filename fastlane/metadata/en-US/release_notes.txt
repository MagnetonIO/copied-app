First release for the Mac App Store.

• Fast menu-bar clipboard manager with keyboard-first navigation
• Full-text search across text, rich text, and HTML
• Smart detection for links, code, images, files, and videos
• Favorites, trash, and custom lists
• Global hotkey (⌃⇧C) opens the popover from any app
• copied:// URL scheme for automation
• Optional iCloud Sync (one-time in-app purchase) keeps history in sync across all your Macs — local history is always free
