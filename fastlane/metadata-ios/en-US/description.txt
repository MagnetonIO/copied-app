Copied is a fast, iCloud-synced clipboard manager for iPhone, iPad, and Mac.

Save anything you copy — text, links, images, code snippets — and have it instantly available on every device signed into your iCloud account. Capture from any app via the Share Extension or the one-tap Copied Clipper action, search your full history, pin favorites, and paste back with full fidelity.

FEATURES
• Share Extension — "Save to Copied" from any app
• Action Extension — one-tap Copied Clipper for fast capture
• Full-text search across text, rich text, and HTML
• Smart content detection for links, code, images, and files
• Code language detection (25+ languages)
• Favorites and trash with time-based retention
• iCloud Sync keeps your clipboard in sync across all your iPhones, iPads, and Macs
• Lists for organizing clippings
• Privacy-first: your data stays in your own iCloud account; never our servers

Designed for writers, developers, and anyone who lives on the clipboard.

Companion to Copied for Mac (sold separately on the Mac App Store).
